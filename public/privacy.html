Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Timezone Clock is a Chrome extension that replaces your new tab page with a real-time multi-timezone world clock. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles your data.

Data We Collect

Timezone Clock does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Network Requests

When you search for a city or timezone, the extension sends your search text to our backend API (timezone-clock-backend-server.vercel.app) to retrieve matching city and timezone results. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.

Local Storage

Your selected cities are saved locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL.
